FAQs (Frequently Asked Questions)

What is Rental buddy?
Rental Buddy is an online platform designed to simplify the process of creating, negotiating, and managing residential lease agreements. Whether you’re a landlord or a tenant, we’re here to make renting hassle-free.
How does Rental buddy work?
Rental Buddy allows property owners to create interactive lease agreements with tenants. Our user-friendly interface guides you through the process, ensuring clarity and compliance with legal requirements.
Can I customise my lease agreements?
Absolutely! You can personalize lease terms, add clauses, and make adjustments based on your specific needs. Rental Buddy’s flexibility ensures that your agreements reflect your unique situation
How do I get started?
Simply sign up, create an account, and start building your lease agreements.
What payment methods are supported for Rental buddy?
Rental Buddy doesn’t handle payments directly. However, you can integrate third-party payment gateways for rent collection.
Is Rental buddy available outside the UK?
Currently, Rental Buddy is designed for the UK rental market. However, we’re exploring expansion to other regions in the future..
How do I invite my tennats to use Rental buddy?
Once you’ve created an agreement, share the link with your tenants. They can review and sign it online, making the process efficient.
